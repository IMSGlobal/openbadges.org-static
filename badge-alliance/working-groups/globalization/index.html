Globalization, Localization & Badge the World Working Group

Between 2014 and 2015, this group encouraged and supported badging in other countries and cultures. Members made badges and badging resources accessible to non-English speakers and supported globalization efforts of other organizations.

Globalization Resources:

The Globalization, Localization & Badge the World mailing list has been archived. Please join us at the Open Badges community space.

The Badge the World site provides the ability for individuals and/or organizations to easily share their badging work, access badging resources, and connect with other badging projects from around the world.
