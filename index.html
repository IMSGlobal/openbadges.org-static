Get Recognition for Skills Earned Anywhere

  • Earn

    Earn badges for skills you learn online and in person.

  • Issue

    Anyone can issue Open Badges to recognize the achievements of others.

  • Display

    Share your badges in places that matter as verifiable records of your learning.

  • Understand

    Verify Open Badges and accept them as records of achievement.

Open Badges may be issued, earned, or authenticated by anyone. They can represent any skill or achievement, from event attendance to professional certification.

Not all Badges are Open Badges

A digital badge is an online representation of a skill youâ€™ve earned, and Open Badges are a subset of digital badges that have powerful additional features. Open Badges are:

  • Free and open: Anyone can use the Open Badges standard to create, issue, and verify digital badges.
  • Transferable: Collect and display your skills and achievements anywhere.
  • Stackable: No matter where they are earned, badges can build upon each other and be stacked.
  • Evidence-based: Each badge has important data encoded into the badge image file that provides verifying evidence.

The Badge Alliance

The Open Badges Specification was developed by the Mozilla Foundation and is maintained by the Badge Alliance, which brings a focus on the shared values of openness, learner agency, and innovation.
